Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly assigned number! You lose the game if your total score goes above that random number!

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new game starts, the crystals will be assigned new number values.
